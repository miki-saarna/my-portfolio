My name is Mikito Saarna, and I am an aspiring software engineer with a diverse background. By reading this portfolio, I hope to impart on you a glimmer of the inspiration that makes up my daily life

The Journey Begins

I grew up in Kansas City for most of my life, but shortly after graduating from high school, I departed my parents' house to attend a Gap-year program called Generation Peace Academy (GPA). This was a very moumental and precious time of my life where I explored different topics such as my own character, spirituality, relationships, my dreams and aspirations and much more. I ended up extending my time in GPA to a total of 3 years

About a year after graduating from GPA, I went on a bike-packing trip from New Jersey to Detroit (I also road through a small portion of Canada). This trip taught me that I can accomplish anything I set my heart and mind to.

I moved to Las Vegas a month after my bike-packing trip and have been living here ever since. I worked with various E-commerce start-ups until I decided to have my career change and study software engineering with Thinkful. During this time I also met and married my wife Misato.
